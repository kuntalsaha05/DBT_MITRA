Let's Get Your Account DBT-Ready

Don't worry, enabling your account for DBT is a straightforward process. Here are two ways to do it.

Online Process (via NPCI)

  1. Go to the official NPCI website.
  2. Click on the 'Consumer' option on the homepage.
  3. From the dropdown, select 'Bharat Aadhaar Seeding Enabler (BASE)'.
  4. Click on the option for 'Aadhaar Seeding'.
  5. Select your bank from the list provided.
  6. Enter your Aadhaar number, account details, and fill the security captcha.
  7. Select the seeding type as 'Fresh Seeding' since you are enabling it for the first time.
  8. You will receive an OTP on your Aadhaar-registered mobile number. Enter it to submit your consent request.

*Please note: After submitting online, your bank will process the request. This may take a few business days.

Offline Process (at Your Bank)

  1. Visit the bank branch where you have your account.
  2. Ask for the 'Aadhaar Seeding and DBT Consent Form'.
  3. Fill out the form with your account details and Aadhaar number.
  4. Attach a self-attested copy of your Aadhaar card.
  5. Submit the form to the bank official. They will give you an acknowledgement receipt.
  6. Your bank will then link your account to the NPCI mapper, making it DBT-enabled.

*This process might take 3-5 working days. You can check your status online after a week.
