How to Check Your DBT Status

It's easy to find out for sure. Use one of these methods to check which bank account is seeded with your Aadhaar.

Method 1: Online via NPCI (Recommended)

The most reliable way is through the official NPCI website.

  1. Go to the official NPCI website.
  2. Click on the 'Consumer' option on the homepage.
  3. From the dropdown, select 'Bharat Aadhaar Seeding Enabler (BASE)'.
  4. Click on the link to 'Get Aadhaar Mapped Status'.
  5. Enter your Aadhaar number and the security captcha.
  6. You will receive an OTP on your registered mobile number. Enter it to view your status.

Method 2: USSD Service

If you don't have internet access, you can use your phone.

  1. Dial *99*99*1# from the mobile number registered with your Aadhaar.
  2. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number.
  3. Confirm the details to see your linked bank account.

What Does Your Status Say?

Based on the result from the NPCI portal, here are your next steps.

If your status is "Active"...

Congratulations! Your account is correctly enabled for DBT. You are all set to receive your scholarship funds.

Learn About Managing Your Account →

If your status is "Inactive" or "No Account"...

This means you need to seed your account. Follow our step-by-step guide to get it done quickly.

See How to Enable Your Account →

Why This is Crucial for Your Scholarship

For schemes like the 'Pre-Matric & Post-Matric scholarships for SC students', the entire scholarship amount is paid ONLY through a DBT-enabled, Aadhaar-seeded bank account.

An account that is only Aadhaar-linked (for KYC) but not seeded for DBT will cause significant delays in receiving your scholarship funds.
