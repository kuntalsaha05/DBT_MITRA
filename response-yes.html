Quick Verification via NPCI

You can double-check your status anytime directly through the NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India) website. This is the most reliable way to confirm which account is active for DBT.

  1. Go to the official NPCI website.
  2. Click on the 'Consumer' option on the homepage.
  3. From the dropdown, select 'Bharat Aadhaar Seeding Enabler (BASE)'.
  4. Click on the link to 'Get Aadhaar Mapped Status'.
  5. Enter your Aadhaar number and the security captcha.
  6. You will receive an OTP on your registered mobile number. Enter it to view your status.

What If Payments Don't Arrive?

If a government scheme payment is delayed, you don't need to worry. We provide a dedicated section on our website with the contact details for the departments responsible for each scheme (e.g., Scholarship office, Pension department). You can find the right contact information there to inquire about your payment status.

Changing Your DBT Account

You can request to change your DBT-linked bank account online through the NPCI portal. This will move the seeding from your old bank to your new one.

  1. Follow the first three steps from the verification process above to get to the 'Bharat Aadhaar Seeding Enabler (BASE)' page.
  2. Click on the option for 'Aadhaar Seeding'.
  3. Select the new bank you want to receive DBT payments in from the list.
  4. Change the seeding type to 'Movement from one bank to another'.
  5. Complete the process by authenticating with your Aadhaar and the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.

Note: This process sends a request to your new bank. The time it takes for the change to be reflected can vary.
