Quick Recap

Aadhaar-Linked: Your Aadhaar is just used as an ID proof (like a Passport or PAN card) for the bank. This does NOT mean you'll get government money in it.

DBT-Enabled (Seeded): You have specifically TOLD the bank to mark this account for receiving government benefits. This is the account that will receive all DBT payments.
